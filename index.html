Assassin Story: A peerless practitioner of lethal shadow magic, Ebon battles to achieve his true destiny—to one day lead the Order of Shadows into a new era of true salvation. During the war, desperation led him to unlock the secret shadow form—a malevolent spirit magic as dangerous and corrupting as it is powerful. Ebon has mastered all of these forbidden techniques to end anything he sees as a threat to his nation, or his new order

Evader story: Among the secretive, Ubela warriors known as the Mist Masters, Nobu serves as their leader, the Eye of Twilight. He longs to remain free from the confusion of emotion, prejudice, and ego, and walks the unseen path of dispassionate judgment between the spirit realm and the physical world. Tasked with enforcing the equilibrium between them, Nobu wields blades of steel and arcane energy against any who would threaten it.

Mage story: Once a prankster Puck has fallen into a crater while walking in a desert. Amidst the ruins of Iotrias, he gazed into the dark heart of the Void and was gifted a power to bring forth the salvation in the name of Void king Draknar. Puck now sees himself as a shepherd, empowered to bring others into the right path.

Tank story: Umak is a terrifying, rage-fueled being from the scorched deserts of Ocaes. Once, he was his empire’s most esteemed warrior, leading the nation’s armies to countless victories. However, due to not being selected to battle against the dragon horde by his commander, he succumbed into anger. After many years of training in seclusion, he is now utterly consumed with finding and killing the last remaining dragon Ignis.
